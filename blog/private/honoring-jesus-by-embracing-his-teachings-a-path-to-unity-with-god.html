Honoring Jesus by Embracing His Teachings: A Path to Unity with God

As I’ve come to understand Jesus more, I’ve started to think about how His life must have felt. He lived with a level of divine understanding and connection that most people couldn’t grasp, and I imagine that, over time, this set Him apart in a profound way. Not because He wanted to separate Himself, but because He had to live and preach in a way that aligned fully with the divine nature He embodied. He wasn’t just teaching people how to live; He was showing them what it meant to be in complete unity with God, something most of us only glimpse in moments of clarity.
