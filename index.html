About

Rooted in rural Oregon, building community spaces and documenting the people who keep them going.

I started restoring an old high school gym at 15, and it became the Ruth Obenchain Recreation Center. Since then, I’ve handled memberships, events, and ongoing restoration while keeping a record of the place and the people around me.

The rest of my story includes line clearance tree trimming, EMT and firefighter training, travel, and caregiving. The full timeline is collected on the About page.
